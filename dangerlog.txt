Danger Log (Open Risk Notes)

1. Request-size abuse risk
   No hard limits on XML payload size or number of child operations per request.
   Potential impact: memory pressure and denial-of-service.

2. Connection idle risk
   Long-lived idle clients are not aggressively evicted.
   Potential impact: worker/file-descriptor exhaustion under malicious or buggy clients.

3. Authentication/authorization scope
   Protocol has account-level operation checks for order/query/cancel ownership,
   but no full client authentication layer (e.g., credentials/session tokens).
   Potential impact: weak security model outside assignment assumptions.
